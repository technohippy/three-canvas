Loaders

At the moment, two loaders are available:

  • THREE.JSONLoader
  • THREE.OBJLoader
To display a loaded object, use a three-loader-mesh element. 

<three-canvas width="200" height="200" antialias="true" clearColor="#000000" defaultLight="true">
  <three-camera position="0,90,800" lookAt="0,90,0" controls="orbit"></three-camera>
  <three-loader-mesh type="obj" src="./vendors/three.js/examples/obj/male02/male02.obj"></three-loader-mesh>
</three-canvas>
